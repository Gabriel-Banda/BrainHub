Quick Answers

Is BrainHub free?
Yes — all features, documents, quizzes, and the AI Tutor are completely free with no hidden costs.
How do I contribute materials?
Select "Contribute Materials" above, fill in the course details and paste a Google Drive or public link to your file. We review and upload within 48 hours.
My university isn't listed?
Send a General Enquiry with your university name and courses. We'll work on adding it as soon as possible.
How often is content added?
We add materials within days of receiving contributions. Check the homepage for the latest updates.
Can I use BrainHub offline?
BrainHub is a PWA — install it on your phone and previously visited pages work offline. Documents need a connection to load.
