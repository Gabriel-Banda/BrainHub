Gabriel J. Banda
Founder & Lead Developer

Gabriel Joshua Banda

Founder · Lead Developer · Designer · Content Author

Gabriel built BrainHub from the ground up — designing every page, writing every line of code, and curating every piece of study content. Driven by a vision to make quality university education accessible and completely free for every Zambian student, he has shipped 55+ features across 5 major releases in just a few months.

From the AI tutor to the gamification system, offline PWA support, multi-language translation engine, Supabase authentication, and 100-question quiz system — every feature was built solo with a relentless focus on student experience.
